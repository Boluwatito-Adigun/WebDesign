Our story

We believe every organisation deserves world-class data

Xorus was founded on a simple conviction: data strategy should not be the exclusive privilege of large enterprises with massive budgets. Every growing business deserves the intelligence to compete.

Built by analysts, for ambitious teams

Xorus was born from years of frustration watching organisations sit on rich datasets while making decisions from gut feel and spreadsheets. Our founders, all practitioners who have built data infrastructure at scale, came together to create something different.

We are not a generic IT consultancy. We are data specialists who combine deep domain expertise with modern engineering to deliver analytics that actually get used by leadership, operations, and product teams alike.

From our base in Nigeria, we work with clients across Africa, Europe, and North America bringing rigour, speed, and genuine partnership to every engagement.

What we stand for

Our values are not wall art. They are the operating principles behind every client engagement.

Radical clarity

We translate complex data into language every stakeholder can act on. If the insight cannot drive a decision, it is not a good insight.

Outcomes first

We measure our success by your results — not the number of dashboards we deliver or models we build. Output means nothing without business impact.

Partnership, not projects

We embed alongside your team, transfer knowledge, and build capability internally. We want to be the last analytics consultancy you need to hire.

Rigour & integrity

We do not overstate results. We do not hide limitations. We give you the honest picture, because good decisions require accurate foundations.

Speed with depth

We deliver first insights in 30 days without sacrificing the depth needed for lasting impact. Fast is only valuable when it is also right.

African excellence, global standard

Built in Africa, built for the world. We bring globally competitive expertise with the contextual intelligence that only comes from working on the continent.
